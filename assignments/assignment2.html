BetaPay is a mobile payments service that enables NFC-based contactless purchases and peer-to-peer money transactions all via smart phone. Perhaps more importantly, it will incorporate many exciting new social features that will distinguish it from competing services. Part of what makes BetaPay unique is that users earn points with every transaction they make, as well as by meeting daily and weekly goals. Accumulating enough points allows a user to "level-up" and earn in-app prizes. BetaPay will also be fully integrated into Beta’s main social media platform, so users of the service will automatically be connected with their Beta friends. Moreover, the points earned by a user's friends are displayed on a leaderboard, enabling friends to compete against each other. Users finishing atop the leaderboard at the end of a period are awarded special badges displayed on their profiles, giving them chance to show off their bragging rights. To make things even more competitive, users have the ability to purchase "boosts". The other major component of the plan is the creation of Beta Marketplace. Since Beta takes a small cut out of all purchases on the Marketplace, it directly benefits from inducing a sale.
